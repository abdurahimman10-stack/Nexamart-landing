Who We Are

Empowering Local Businesses, Simplifying Shopping

NexaMart is a progressive digital marketplace connecting customers with trusted local businesses on one easy-to-use platform. Instead of managing a private centralized warehouse, NexaMart partners directly with neighborhood shops, supermarkets, pharmacies, restaurants, and apparel outlets to showcase their inventory live online.

Our Mission

To help local businesses expand exponentially by bringing them online while making shopping remarkably faster, safer, and more convenient for consumers.

Our Vision

To establish NexaMart as Ethiopia's most relied-upon online marketplace and hyper-local delivery network, bridging millions of shoppers with businesses nationwide.
